About VEEMACK Technical Services

VEEMACK Technical Services is a team of certified engineers and technicians with over a decade of experience in industrial automation, power systems, and electrical control engineering. Our mission is to deliver safe, efficient, and durable panel solutions that power industries with confidence. From concept to commissioning, we ensure that every system meets the highest global standards.

Our Expertise

Custom Panel Design

Our engineers design panels tailored to your production environment, integrating precise load calculations, component layouts, and ventilation management. Each design undergoes simulation tests before fabrication to ensure performance and safety compliance.

Wiring & Testing

Every connection is made with certified cables and labeled for traceability. We perform insulation resistance tests, continuity checks, and phase balancing to guarantee reliability under heavy loads.

Installation & Maintenance

We handle on-site installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance with full adherence to safety procedures and verified control logic performance.

Automation & Control Systems

We integrate PLCs, sensors, relays, and HMIs for smart control. Every control logic is tested in simulation before deployment to ensure seamless operation.

Our Projects

Each VEEMACK panel reflects precision and safety. We carefully plan layouts for heat dissipation, circuit clarity, and maintenance access. Every wire is labeled, every terminal is tested, and every panel passes final inspection.

Main Distribution Panel

Built with robust copper busbars and color-coded insulation for three-phase distribution. Each section is torque-tested and heat-verified for industrial safety.

Power Control Center

A masterfully arranged PCC with MCCBs, relays, and clean busbar routing. Components are calibrated and continuity-tested to ensure zero fault tolerance.

Automation Panel

Equipped with PLCs, contactors, and relays, each module is pre-programmed, load-tested, and documented for diagnostics and smooth client handover.
